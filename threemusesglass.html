Project Description

In 2015 or so, I wanted to make an online store for my mom. She never asked me to, or gave me any feedback during the process so it became a purely acedemic exercise. Even so, I managed to create a (at one time) fully functioning online store. It had a cart, emailed reciepts, calculated shipping costs with postmark, took sandbox payments using both paypal and stripe, had admin pages for inventory management, the ability to allow users to create an account after their purchase was complete, user login with purchase histories, and probably more. Many of the API integrations have since decayed at the unforgiving hands of time, but here is a link to the legacy project if you're feeling brave enough.
